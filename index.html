About us

As a social enterprise, ECAA supporting local development organisations to access digital funds for their charitable projects and to use them being in Africa. We are a digital fundraiser to support charitable projects across the Africa continent.

Approach

Our first approach is to store digital funds donated by people with the aim to convert them into local currencies by using different ways (local buyers, online platforms, etc.). The second approach is to promote the use of digital funds locally with the aim to make them more convertible and usable by stakeholders.

Missions and Values

Our mission is to facilitate transactions for funders, investors, public or private users who aim to expend Ethereum to develop Africa. We raise communities awareness, we train, we build partnerships, we innovate buy sell transfer and store currency. We share and promote the following values: 1. Positive energy 2. Clear communication 3. Continues learning 4. Honest and sustainable action
